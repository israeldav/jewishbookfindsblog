Some of the most popular paraphernalia from Jewish soldiers serving in WW I and WW II (and the Korean war?) are books that were printed by the JWB (Jewish Welfare Board) and distributed to Jewish military personnel.  Siddurim, Machzorim, Tanachs, Haggadahs, Megillas and many other books/pamphlets/Jewish guides were printed by the thousands and are fairly easy to pick up today. If you're lucky, you can find one with a name & rank or some note written on the inside cover.
